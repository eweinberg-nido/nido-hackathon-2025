AIDeatED

Building the Future of AI in Schools

May 8–9 2026

Hosted by
Santiago, Chile

AI is transforming education. In this conference, we will focus on how we can actualize that transformation through building effective and meaningful AI designed tools for our classrooms.

Rather than attending traditional workshops or listening to polished keynotes, educators select a strand and work alongside peers examining shared questions, constraints, and possibilities.

Over two days, participants will engage in focused, collaborative design.

The Concept

Less Presentation, More Creation

Many AI conferences focus on ideas. AIdeatED is about dedicated time to act on them. You'll choose a dilemma, join or create a team, and spend two days building something you can take back to your school. Inspiration sessions set the tone, but the work happens in the room with other educators like you, with expert support nearby when you need it.

Examples of what you might leave with:

  • A working prototype, resource, or framework you helped create
  • Connections with educators who share your questions
  • Practical experience and time using AI tools to build

This might be a parent communication guide, an app for tracking anecdotal student notes, a system for ensuring mixed groups, or a set of rational policies for using AI in a specific subject area. What you build depends on what your team decides to tackle during the event.
