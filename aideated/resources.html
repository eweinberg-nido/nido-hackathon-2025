Prompting & Context

Strong results come from giving an AI teammate the background, constraints, preferences, and examples it needs to make good decisions.

Hiring an AI teammate

SPARK Framework

Use SPARK to move beyond simple input-output prompting and clearly define the work you want an AI teammate to help with.

S

Situation

Describe the current state or context in which you are operating.

P

Problem

Identify the specific problem or challenge you are facing.

A

Aspiration

Articulate your desired outcome or vision for success.

R

Results

Define the specific, measurable results you hope to achieve.

K

Kismet

Add an element of serendipity, surprise, or creative possibility.

Useful context

What to give the AI

Content is only part of the work. The AI also needs the implicit structure behind your decisions.

  • Knowledge
  • Information
  • Data
  • Priorities
  • Style guide
  • Document formats

Talk it through first

For some people, speaking through the SPARK stages is more effective than writing a prompt from scratch. Record your thinking, then paste or upload the transcript into the AI tool you are using.
