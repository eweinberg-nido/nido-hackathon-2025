Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

+
Our hackathon is open to everyone, regardless of skill level or background. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a complete beginner, you're welcome to join!

Do I need a team?

+
You can register as an individual or in a team of up to four. We will have a dedicated session for team formation at the start of the event.

What should I bring?

+
You should bring your own laptop, charger, and any other devices you need to work. We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable working environment.
