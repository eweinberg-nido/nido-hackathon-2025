How to Demo

A practical structure and checklist to deliver a clean, confident hackathon demo.

Core demo structure

Hook

Open with one line that captures attention.

Problem + persona

Explain who has the problem and why it matters.

Demo wow features

Show your 1-3 strongest features live.

Reminder

Keep it similar to this presentation style: clear, visual, and not too much text.

Presentation structure

STEP 01

Hook

Open with one line that captures attention.

STEP 02

Problem + persona

Explain who has the problem and why it matters.

STEP 03

Demo wow features

Show your 1-3 strongest features live.

STEP 04

How it works

Give a brief technical explanation only.

STEP 05

Profitability + growth

Show potential traction, users, and scaling logic.

STEP 06

Next steps

Close with what comes next for your market.

Delivery tips

Rehearse, rehearse, rehearse

Practice multiple times with timing and role handoffs.

Reduce live typing risk

Avoid relying on lots of last-second input during the pitch.

Backup strategy

Have seeded data, screenshots, and a short backup video ready in case of issues.

Slide style

  • Minimal text: one idea per slide
  • Visual proof: product screens over paragraphs
  • Consistent flow: problem → demo → value
  • Strong close: market + next step
