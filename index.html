ABOUT

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people collaborate to develop creative solutions to real-world problems in a limited time. Teams work on projects related to STEM, AI, Coding, Entrepreneurship, and Design, using cutting-edge technology, innovation, and teamwork to bring ideas to life.

At Nido Hackathon 2025, participants will brainstorm, prototype, and present their projects while getting mentorship and access to industry tools.

Theme: For Students By Students: Leveraging AI Innovation for Health

This hackathon is open to all high school students at Nido, regardless of experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder/designer, this is your chance to learn, create, and connect.

No coding experience? No problem! We’ll have AI and coding tool training to help you get started.

Why Join?

  • Innovate & Solve – Work on exciting challenges in STEM, AI, business, and problem-solving.
  • Learn & Collaborate – Gain hands-on experience, meet mentors, and work in teams.
  • Use AI & Tech Tools – Experiment with AI, coding platforms, and prototyping software.
  • Win Exciting Prizes – Compete for prizes that recognize creativity and innovation.
  • Free Food & Snacks – Stay energized with sponsored meals and refreshments!
