ABOUT THE HACKATHON

Our Goal & Theme

Our core goal is to empower high school students to explore STEM, leverage AI, and turn their ideas into reality. This year's theme is Health, and we're bringing students together to solve real-world problems in this crucial field.

This hackathon is open to all high school students, regardless of experience. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a future entrepreneur, or a passionate health advocate, this is your chance to learn, create, and connect. No coding experience? No problem!

Why Join?

  • Solve Real Problems: Tackle meaningful challenges in the health sector.
  • Build Your Skills: Learn to create powerful applications and MVPs.
  • Learn from Experts: Gain hands-on experience and meet mentors.
  • Win Exciting Prizes: Compete for awards that recognize innovation.
  • Free Food & Snacks: Stay energized with sponsored meals!

Our Mentors & Workshops

We are proud to have mentors with expertise in Computer Science/AI, Business, and Health. They will be available to guide you throughout the event. We will also host workshops covering key topics in each of these three areas to help you build your project.

Technology Experts

Mentors and workshops focused on CS and AI to help you build powerful applications.

Business Entrepreneurs

Learn how to pitch, find market fit, and build a business from the ground up.

Health Advocates

Gain insights into real-world health problems and develop impactful solutions.
